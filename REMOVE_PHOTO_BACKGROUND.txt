🎯 REMOVE BACKGROUND FROM YOUR PROFILE PHOTO
=============================================

I can see your profile photo has a brown/beige background that needs to be removed.

EASIEST METHOD (Takes 10 seconds):

1. Go to: https://www.remove.bg/

2. Click "Upload Image"

3. Select your current profile photo from:
   cosmic-portfolio/public/profile.png

4. Wait 5 seconds - it will automatically remove the brown background

5. Click "Download HD" or "Download" button

6. Save the downloaded file

7. Replace the old file:
   - Delete: cosmic-portfolio/public/profile.png
   - Rename your downloaded file to: profile.png
   - Move it to: cosmic-portfolio/public/

8. Refresh your browser at http://localhost:8080/

RESULT:
Your photo will have a transparent background instead of the brown one,
making it look more professional with the cosmic theme!

ALTERNATIVE TOOLS:
- Photopea: https://www.photopea.com/ (Free Photoshop)
- Canva: https://www.canva.com/ (Background Remover tool)
- Adobe Express: https://www.adobe.com/express/feature/image/remove-background

The transparent background will blend perfectly with your portfolio's
dark cosmic theme and animated rings! ✨
